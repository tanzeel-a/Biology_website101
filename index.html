Milestones of Life

1665

Discovery of the Cell

Robert Hooke observes cork under a microscope and coins the term "cell".

1859

Evolution by Natural Selection

Charles Darwin publishes "On the Origin of Species", revolutionizing our understanding of life's diversity.

1866

Mendelian Genetics

Gregor Mendel discovers the fundamental laws of inheritance through pea plant experiments.

1953

Structure of DNA

Watson, Crick, and Franklin reveal the double helix structure of DNA. (Nobel Prize 1962)

1983

PCR Invention

Kary Mullis invents Polymerase Chain Reaction, allowing rapid DNA amplification. (Nobel Prize 1993)

2003

Human Genome Project

Completion of the full sequencing of the human genome.

2012

CRISPR-Cas9

Doudna and Charpentier develop a revolutionary method for genome editing. (Nobel Prize 2020)
